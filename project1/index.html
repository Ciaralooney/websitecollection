The Amazing World of Gumball first aired on Cartoon Network in 2011. It follows the life of Gumball Watterson, Darwin Watterson and their many friends and family.

The Watterson family has a flair for misadventure and with each episode we see how they traverse through a newfound problem in their hometown of Elmore.

Gumball Watterson; a 12-year-old cat, is the main character of the series. Below are a picture of a few of his friends.
