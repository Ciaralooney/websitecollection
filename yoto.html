Yoto Games

The Yoto player is a screen-free audio player for kids. You insert a physical card and the player streams audio with 2 buttons as the only inputs. I designed and built two interactive games for it. Each game is linked to a physical card that triggers a live Python server. Designing for a device with no screen meant every piece of feedback and choice made had to work through audio alone.

Trivia Time

A screen-free trivia game streamed live to a physical Yoto card. Questions are pulled at random each round, so no two plays of the card sound the same. The player gets instant feedback on their choices and a final score reveal.

Platform: Yoto player

Genre: Audio game, trivia · Ages 8 to 12

Team: Solo

My roles: Game Designer, Programmer

Jungle Adventure

A branching audio adventure told through the Yoto's buttons and speaker. Three stories with multiple decisions in each, so the same card ends differently every time you play it.

Platform: Yoto player

Genre: Audio game, choose your own adventure · Ages 6 to 9

Team: Solo

My roles: Game Designer, Programmer, Writer
