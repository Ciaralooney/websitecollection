This page tracks my level design work for an upcoming 2D platformer I am working on. This is a 5 level project built specifically to showcase level design as its own discipline. Work has started with greyboxing, building out the rough layout and pacing of each level before any art or polish goes in. Careful playtesting will take place during the greyboxing stage to ensure the level design is high quality and even during this stripped back stage the core mechanics of the game's fun is clear to see.

Greyboxing lets me test how a level actually plays before committing to visuals. I will be able to test things like spacing, sightlines, difficulty pacing across the 5 levels in depth and make adjustments where needed. This page will update with screenshots and footage as that process plays out.

Design

The project is structured as 5 levels with a deliberate difficulty curve, each one introduces or reinforces a specific mechanic before the next level builds on it. This is a different kind of curve to the one in Smooth Snake, where difficulty emerged naturally from one accumulating rule. Instead the curve is directly because of the layout of the level.

Level 1

This is the first design for level 1. Level 1 teaches wall jumping in a safe space with little risk if the player doesn't commit, giving them time to learn.

Expected Player Movement

The player begins the level on the left and will soon begin to wall jump. When they reach the top there is a small platform they can stand on and see what's ahead. If they fall they can still land on the ground below. But if they make a calculated jump they can clear the pit below. The player will see there is a hazard in their way so they jump onto the platform just above. The player jumps up to the next ascending platform in order to avoid the pit below. Level End. This level will be improved and expanded on after playtesting.

Update: After playtesting I have begun to tweak the level as testers consistently missed the wall jump timing on the first attempt, so I widened the left platform to give them a chance to land. I also included a visual cue on the right platform that players can see once they clear the wall jump.

I recieved feedback that the level is too short so I started to expand the level. I added places for the player to explore and rewards if they do so like if the player chooses to explore after the wall jump (walk left) they are rewarded with another coin.


Level 4

I wanted to design a later level as I believe it will be easier to adapt the difficult curve needed for the levels inbetween. This is just the start of the level. It will be adapted after playtesting and expanded upon in time

Update: I have done some playtesting for this level and recieved some very helpful feedback. I was told the level is too short so I expanded it. I also had to move some of the grey platforms around a bit as players were having problems navigating around them.

I have recieved more feedback on this level. The area below the secret area is quite long and nothing happens there. I was told that during the 2nd last wall jump it is very hard to land on the top of a platform when you reach the top.

Actions taken: I have added an extra hazard, small platform and reward to the large empty area to vary it a bit. I was happy to hear the feedback about the wall jump as this is intentional but I saw how much the testers struggled with it so I will add a small platform to make it a little easier. I don't want to add a platform that's too long as players may be able to run and jump over the next wall jump challenge.

After Feedback this is the final level draft:

Expected Player Movement

The player begins the level on the left and will start walking right. When they see the 2 walls and no other way to progress they will wall jump until they reach the top. The explore by walking left and find a coin. They then jump over the hole and proceed forward. They jump over the hazard and try to land on the middle wall platform. They miss and begin to wall jump out of the bottomless pit. They succeed and are back on the platform they wer eon. They try to jump again and this time land it and get a coin. They carefully drag themselves down the wall facing them and wall jump at the last second, they wall jump again and carefully land on the small platform. They navigate the small grey platforms and jump upwards, grabbing the coin before leaving the area. They jump down and notice a crack on the wall. They break it and it reveals a small hidden area with many coins. They gather the coins and then waslk right, they jump to avoid the hazards below. They see there is coins behind them so they walk left and jump over the hazard and land on the platofrm to grab the coins. After this they proceed right and begin to wall jump again. The player lands on the middle wall with the coin on it. They try to jump from here to the final platofrm but miss, so they wall jump one last time to get out of the pit and are able to clear the landing from here.
